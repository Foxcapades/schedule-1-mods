= Schedule I Mods

A collection of tiny mods to tweak to the gameplay experience.

[cols="2,1,7"]
|===
| Mod | Type | Description

| link:CopHealthModifier/[Cop Health Modifier]
| Difficulty
| Adds a configurable multiplier value to adjust police max health.

| link:DayLengthModifier/[Day Length Modifier]
| Difficulty
| Adds a configurable multiplier value to adjust game day length.

| link:DisableDealBonuses/[Disable Deal Bonuses]
| Difficulty
| Removes the bonus payments on deals for quality, curfew, quick delivery, and
  weather.

| link:DonutMixFix/[Donut Mixing Fix]
| Fix
| Corrects the mixing interaction with `CalorieDense + CalorieDense`.

| link:GrowthSpeedModifier/[Plant Growth Modifiers]
| Difficulty
| Adds configurable multiplier values to adjust the growth speed of different
  plant types.

| link:GunControl/[Gun Control]
| Difficulty
| Marks guns and ammunition as illegal.

| link:IllegalSeeds/[Illegal Seeds]
| Difficulty
| Marks coca and marijuana seeds as illegal.

| link:ObservantCops/[Observant Cops]
| Difficulty
| Makes cops notice when the player is openly holding an illegal item.

| link:RecommendedPrice/[Recommended Price]
| Difficulty
| Adds configurable multiplier values to adjust the market price of the
  different drug types.
|===
