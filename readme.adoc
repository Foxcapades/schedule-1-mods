= Schedule I Mods

* link:CopHealthModifier/[Cop Health Modifier] - Adds a configurable multiplier
  value to adjust police max health.
* link:DayLengthModifier/[Day Length Modifier] - Adds a configurable multiplier
  value to adjust game day length.
* link:DisableDealBonuses/[Disable Deal Bonuses] - Removes the bonus payments
  on deals for quality, curfew, quick delivery, and weather.
* link:DonutMixFix/[Donut Mixing Fix] - Corrects the mixing interaction with
  `CalorieDense + CalorieDense`.
* link:GrowthSpeedModifier/[Plant Growth Speed Modifiers] - Adds configurable
  multiplier values per plant type to adjust the growth speed of those plants.
* link:GunControl/[Gun Control] - Marks guns and ammunition as illegal.
* link:IllegalSeeds/[Illegal Seeds] - Marks coca and marijuana seeds as illegal.
* link:ObservantCops/[Observant Cops] - Makes cops notice when the player is
  openly holding an illegal item.
* link:RecommendedPrice/[Recommended Price] - Adds configurable multiplier
  values to adjust the market price of the different drug types.
